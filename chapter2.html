Chapter 2: The Scholar’s Notes

Lincoln sat in his dimly lit dorm room, the glow of his laptop screen casting long shadows on the walls. His discovery from the ancient manuscript had led him here—to an obscure reference in the notes of a long-forgotten biblical scholar, **Dr. Elias Holden**.

Lincoln’s search had brought him to a cryptography forum, where a user claiming to have access to Holden’s old research had posted a fragment of one of his letters. If Holden had encrypted a message, it was up to Lincoln to crack the code.
